about=This plugin provides functionality to convert between NASA's Unified Metadata Model (UMM), STAC (SpatioTemporal Asset Catalog), and DQ4EO (Data Quality for Earth Observation) formats. It supports four conversion modes: UMM↔STAC and UMM↔DQ4EO. The plugin includes full support for quality information conversion, mapping UMM DataQuality fields to ISO 19115/19157-compliant quality reports in DQ4EO format.
